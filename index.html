About Lawrence Auto Mobile

I personally source vehicles from trusted U.S. dealerships, inspect them, arrange purchase, and coordinate secure international shipping for clients worldwide. Fast communication, transparent quotes, and experience dealing with export logistics.

  • Personal sourcing and negotiation
  • Pre-purchase inspection and paperwork
  • Shipping logistics and customs support

Our Full Process

We handle every step end-to-end so you receive your vehicle smoothly and securely.

1

Order Car

Tell us the make, model, year and any options you want — we’ll start sourcing and negotiate the best deal with trusted dealerships.

2

Inspection

We perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection (mechanical, cosmetic, and paperwork) and send you a detailed report and photos.

3

Payment

We handle safe payment processing and escrow options; you receive a full invoice and purchase documentation.

4

Shipping

We arrange secure international shipping, choose the best carrier (RO-RO, container), and provide tracking updates.

5

Clearance

We assist with customs clearance paperwork and local import requirements to make release at the destination as smooth as possible.

6

Pickup

Once cleared, the vehicle is available for pickup or last-mile delivery; we confirm handover and close the transaction.
